About the gameplay This is co-op minesweeper. Each player in a game has their own separate minefield to solve, but they can only see numbers for squares that all players have opened. Try to work together in the same area of the minefield to reveal information for everyone.
Controls and ending the game
  • Click to open a square. If it's a bomb, you'll know. Otherwise, it may remain unkown for a while.
  • Right-click to flag a square, marking it as a suspected bomb.
  • Keep playing. The game doesn't tell you when you've won or lost.
